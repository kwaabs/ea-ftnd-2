Option B

Custom SVG Sankey (proper proportional ribbons + labels)

Hand-built so we control everything recharts won't: true proportional link widths, on-node labels + values, semantic colours, gradient ribbons, hover highlight.

Option C

Supply / demand balance bars (compact, no SVG)

The simplest, most robust option: two stacked proportional bars (where supply comes from / where it goes) plus KPI chips. Reads instantly, trivial to maintain, mobile-friendly.
